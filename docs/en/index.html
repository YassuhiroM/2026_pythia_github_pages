Pythia

Data Science for Cafeterias

Final project for the Master in Data Science at UCM/NTIC.

Real world project Uses real sales and customer review data from a Madrid coffee shop to build a practical, intelligent cafeteria application.

Integrates distinct fields of knowledge Effectively combines coursework in ETL, EDA, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Time Series, NLP, and advanced visualization into one coherent solution.

Built as a real product Presented as a functioning MVP in Streamlit, showing the project as a usable product rather than only an academic deliverable.

Recognitions Received the maximum grade and earned 2nd place in a pitch contest.

Why Pythia

Pythia turns a complex data science challenge into a coherent product story. The project combines rigorous methodology, explainable modeling choices, and a polished end-to-end experience so recruiters, professors, and technical evaluators can understand both the business value and the engineering quality in less than three minutes.
